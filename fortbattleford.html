Fort Battleford

An illustrated map of Fort Battleford was the goal of this project. The format is a trifold brochure with a title page, map, and location markers with descriptions.

Process

A spooky theme is illustrated with pixelated graphics of ghosts and map elements to add intrigue to the piece. A cool teal contrasted with a bright red creates the atmosphere of a spooky forest.
