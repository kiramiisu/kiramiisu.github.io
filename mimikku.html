Mimikku

The Mimikku project is an app to help users learn Japanese onomatopoeia through a fun interactive rhythm game!

Process

Mimikku is the Japanese work for mimic, a play on the fact onomatopoeia words aim to mimic a sound or feeling. The style of illustration is messy and expressive for approchability and playfulness. Analogous colours are calming yet vibraint enough to keep users attention.
