Beverage Redesign

Ramune

The goal for the beverage redesign project is chosing a drink label to redesign, along with any additions to the packaging. I chose to do Sangaria Ramune, specifically the original flavour.

Process

The original flavour of ramune is most similar to a lemon‐lime soda. Lemon imagery is used for the front of the bottle with sparkles sprinkled around. There is a holographic effect applied to the glass bottle giving the soda a unique appearence.
